Description

This app allows you to take images from your device and generate a line-drawing map based on that picture. Choose from your gallery, previous maps made by the app, or take a picture with your camera! From there, bounce a ball on the map and watch it collide with the image. There will be a selection of base images to choose from as well.

The purpose of the app is to utilize user creativity for unlimited content and fun. The premise of map generation can be later used in future apps. Also, this app's simple collision detection could be utilized in future applications by the team. We want the users of our app to have a blast trying out different photos and even drawing their own maps! The possibilities are endless with this app.

We at Photoball are excited for users to test their creativity through our app. It was an awesome experience creating this app, and letting users now get to use it makes it even more exciting!
